Overview

Bridging environmental perception and wireless communication.

V2X-SenseComm is designed to support learning-based research at the intersection of vehicular sensing, ray-tracing communication, and beam management. It provides a structured data pipeline that connects sensing observations with communication channels and beam labels.

Multimodal Sensing

Radar observations and extensible interfaces for future vision, LiDAR, and positioning modalities.

Ray-Tracing Channels

Communication channels generated from geometry-aware V2X scenarios.

Beam Labels

Predefined codebook labels for learning-based beam prediction and beam management.

Expandable Platform

Designed for future datasets, tasks, baselines, papers, and open-source releases.

Dataset Generation

From V2X scene construction to learning-ready beam labels.

01

Scenario Construction

Roads, buildings, target vehicles, obstacle vehicles, and infrastructure nodes.

02

Sensing Simulation

Radar observations are generated from vehicle-centric or infrastructure-assisted sensing setups.

03

Communication Ray Tracing

V2I channels are simulated with geometry-aware propagation and dynamic blockers.

04

Beam Label Generation

Optimal beam indices or beam heatmaps are generated from predefined codebooks.

Learning Benchmark

Dataset

Organized for reproducible sensing-assisted communication research.

V2X-SenseComm/
├── scenes/                 # Scenario descriptions and metadata
├── sensing/                # Radar observations and sensing features
├── communication/          # Ray-tracing channels and beam responses
├── labels/                 # Beam indices, beam maps, or heatmap labels
├── configs/                # Simulation and codebook configurations
├── scripts/                # Data generation and preprocessing scripts
└── README.md

ML Tasks

Initial and future research tasks supported by V2X-SenseComm.

Radar-Aided Beam Prediction

Predict the optimal communication beam using radar angular spectra or radar-derived representations.

Beam Map Learning

Learn spatial beam heatmaps that connect sensing observations with fine-grained beam spaces.

Near-Field Beam Management

Support XL-MIMO beam training under mixed near-field and far-field propagation conditions.
